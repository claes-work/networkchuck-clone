# NetworkChuck (YouTube channel / brand)

The flagship YouTube channel and personal brand of [the subject] **Chuck Keith** — free IT
training that grew into an education-media-coffee business. The "NetworkChuck" name is now the
umbrella for the [[networkchuck-coffee|coffee company]], [[networkchuck-academy|Academy]], and
[[networkchuck-cloud-browser|Cloud Browser]], all operated under **NetworkChuck LLC** (Texas).

## History

- **Oct 2014** — first public video: setting up **Cisco firewalls in an office IT closet**,
  filmed at his job. [🗣️/📰 Grokipedia; Raspberry Pi Official Magazine interview]
- **Inspiration:** a YouTuber named **Jorge (Almazan)** documenting his own IT career — the
  only person Chuck could find "talking about tech that way." [🗣️ Raspberry Pi interview]
- **~2018–2020 pivot:** from niche **Cisco collaboration/VoIP** content to broad **IT-career
  guidance, certification prep (CCNA, CompTIA, AWS), Linux, cybersecurity/ethical hacking, and
  homelab** hands-on tutorials — the format that drove rapid growth. [📰 CBT Nuggets; Favikon]
  > ⚠️ Common error: the channel is sometimes said to have "started in 2018." It was
  > **created in 2014**; ~2018 is the growth-pivot era, not the start.

## Content philosophy [📰/🗣️]

Make IT **fun and unintimidating** — "you CAN do this." Hands-on labs you can replicate,
humor, an ever-present coffee ritual, and the removal of gatekeeping so anyone can launch an
IT career without a traditional degree (certifications + practice). Favikon: "making IT
education both effective and fun … using humor and real-world applications."
