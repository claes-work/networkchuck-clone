## Relationship to NetworkChuck [📰]

- Featured as the guest hacker in NetworkChuck's video **"he hacked my websites"**
  (2024-09-18). Chuck — who states he is "not an ethical hacker" and does only the
  automated/beginner tooling himself — hands the sites over to Tyler, "a real ethical
  hacker, a real pen tester," to do the manual exploitation.
  [[../sources/2024-09-18-yt-AxMWywGFSfs]]
- With **Chuck's explicit authorization** and only on Chuck's **own** sites, Tyler ran a
  real external-pentest workflow (GoWitness, Nuclei, Caido, dirsearch) and demonstrated
  manual attacks: reflected/SVG-based XSS, WordPress username enumeration, an exposed
  `.env` file leaking DB/root passwords, an admin-panel bypass (admin/admin), an API-key
  denial-of-service, and an open-redirect turned into XSS.
  [[../sources/2024-09-18-yt-AxMWywGFSfs]]
- A recurring teaching point of his in the video: "the difference between a pen tester and
  someone who ends up in prison is pen testers pay attention to scope" — out-of-scope
  hosts stay off-limits even when a vulnerability is discovered.
