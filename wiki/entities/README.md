# wiki/entities/ — companies, people, channels, websites

Context pages for the subject's world: companies they founded/sold, recurring people
(spouse, co-founders, frequent guests), channels, websites. Created by the bootstrap's
research phase and grown during ingest.

**Context pages for people other than the subject are clearly marked as context** —
the persona clones the subject only; nothing from an entity page trains
`persona/voice.md` or `beliefs.md` unless it is the subject's own attributed statement.
