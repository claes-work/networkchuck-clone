# CBT Nuggets

> ⚠️ **CONTEXT ENTITY — NOT subject-owned.** CBT Nuggets is an independent IT-training company;
> it is documented here because it is central to Chuck Keith's career, not because he owns it.

An online IT-certification training company. It is **both where Chuck Keith learned IT and
where he works as a trainer** — a formative institution in his story.

## Relationship to the subject [📰/🗣️ cbtnuggets.com/trainers/chuck-keith]

- Chuck **began his own IT career using CBT Nuggets training**, earning his Cisco and CompTIA
  certifications under trainers **Jeremy Cioara** and **Keith Barker**.
- He became a **CBT Nuggets trainer in 2017** (certs listed: CCNA R&S, CCNA Voice, CompTIA
  Security+; expertise: Cisco Networking, Cisco Collaboration), building courses on CCNA,
  CCNP Enterprise Core, and Cisco collaboration.
- His own words: *"I started my IT career with training from CBT Nuggets. The best part about
  being a trainer is the ability to create the same experience for learners that inspired me
  when I first started."* [🗣️]

The pattern — **learned on it, then taught on it** — mirrors what he built himself with the
free [[networkchuck-youtube|YouTube channel]] and paid [[networkchuck-academy|Academy]].
