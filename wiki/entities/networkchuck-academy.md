# NetworkChuck Academy

[The subject] **Chuck Keith's** paid online-learning platform — the membership business built
on top of the free [[networkchuck-youtube|YouTube channel]]. Tagline: **"dedicated to helping
you hack your IT career."**

## What it is [🗣️ academy.networkchuck.com/about]

- **Mission:** help people become **network engineers, ethical hackers, cloud engineers, and
  IT professionals** and "reach their IT goals"; grew out of the ~2.8M-subscriber YouTube
  audience. [🗣️]
- **Model:** membership/subscription. Aggregators report **~$12/month for access to 700+
  lessons**, plus a **"Weekly Skill Drop."** [📰 creator-stat / net-worth sites — pricing is
  indicative, not audited]
- **Content/services:** structured courses (certification paths: CCNA, CompTIA, cloud, Linux,
  cybersecurity), weekly skill-building drops, and community. Support: hello@networkchuck.com.

## Timing [🗣️/📰]

Launched/branded around **2021–2022** — a public LinkedIn push for "Network Chuck – Hack Your
IT Career" dates to **~April 2022**
(linkedin.com/posts/chuckkeith_network-chuck-hack-your-it-career-activity-6911744788270624768-9Iz0).
Exact incorporation/launch date not pinned.

## Leadership [🗣️ stated-in-source]

In a 2025-07-28 interview ([[../sources/2025-07-28-yt-2jgDUaF8E8M]]), Chuck describes his
formative CBT Nuggets mentor **[[jeremy-cioara|Jeremy Cioara]]** as a **NetworkChuck Academy
co-founder** — the same mentor whose on-camera teaching style Chuck says he modeled his own on.
Marked **self-reported / stated-in-source** (not independently registry-verified).

## Philosophy

Same as the channel: **make IT approachable, hands-on, and "you CAN do this"** — a paid,
structured extension of the free content, aimed at people launching tech careers **without a
traditional degree** (certifications + practice).
