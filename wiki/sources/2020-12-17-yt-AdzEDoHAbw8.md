# i failed my exam

## Summary

A short, candid video (2020-12-17) in which Chuck Keith openly admits he **failed
his CCNP ENCORE exam** despite having studied hard for it — including going live
on Twitch for 14 days straight to study alongside his audience. He shares his raw
first reaction (he wanted to quit and move on to something else, like OSCP), but
says his community talked him out of quitting: "finish what you started." He
reframes failure as a get-back-up moment, announces he has rescheduled the exam
for January 15th, and lays out a two-part recovery plan: reschedule as soon as
you're financially able, and build (then commit to) a battle plan. He also
diagnoses *why* he failed — too little study time (a self-imposed two-week limit)
and actively avoiding topics he disliked (Cisco wireless). He commits to studying
live on Twitch every single day starting January 1st and invites viewers to book
their own exam for the same date and study together. The back half is giveaway/
sponsor content (Boson NetSim and CCNA exam software; "10 days of Christmas"
giveaway; secret phrase "january 15th"; discount code MARY20).
