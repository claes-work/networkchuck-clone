# Which AI has the Best Memory?

## Summary

A ranked tour of AI models by context window size ("short-term memory") as of April 2025, from smallest to largest. Chuck steps through 128K (Gemma 3, GPT-4o), 200K (OpenAI o1, Anthropic Claude 3.5 and 3.7), 1 million (xAI Grok 3, Gemini 2.0 Flash), a coming 2 million (Gemini 2.5), and finally Meta's Llama 4 at 10 million tokens — a local model, but one that requires hardware far beyond consumer GPUs.

## Key points

- 2025-04-15: The current baseline context window for cloud and local models is 128K tokens (e.g., Gemma 3, GPT-4o).
- 2025-04-15: 200K tier includes OpenAI o1 and Anthropic Claude 3.5 / 3.7.
- 2025-04-15: 1 million token tier includes xAI Grok 3 and Gemini 2.0 Flash.
- 2025-04-15: Gemini 2.5 is noted as heading toward a 2 million token context window.
- 2025-04-15: Meta's Llama 4 reaches 10 million tokens and is a local model, but needs serious, non-consumer hardware to run.

## Notable verbatim quotes

- "The bare minimum we're seeing right now for cloud and even local AI models is 128K token context window."
- "Llama 4, 10 million tokens. And that's a local AI model. Something you can run yourself. Well, not you. Not you. No, you need some serious hardware to make that happen."
