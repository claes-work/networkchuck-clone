# Is NetworkChuck Over!?!?!

## Summary

A channel-meta update video Chuck posted after a long publishing gap. He addresses head-on the question of whether the NetworkChuck channel is ending, and answers with an emphatic no. The gap, he explains, is because he recently started working full-time with training company [[../entities/cbt-nuggets|CBT Nuggets]] — he quit his nine-to-five as a network engineer / voice engineer to go full-time at CBT, starting January 1st (2018). Adjusting to the new job and producing CBT "nuggets" ate his time, so the channel went dark, but he frames the CBT role as NetworkChuck and CBT Nuggets "joining forces": his new job is literally to study and teach, giving him more material for the channel.

He also rebuts a viewer concern that he "sold out" to CBT Nuggets — insisting his recommendations aren't biased just because CBT is now his employer, because he used and loved CBT Nuggets (learning IT from trainers Jeremy Cioara, Keith Barker, Anthony Sequeira) before they hired him. He makes a public commitment to upload at least one video a week, describes the content mix to come (IT-career advice, study hacks, which certifications to pursue, technical tutorials across networking/security/collaboration), thanks the community, and teases upcoming career-focused videos and a Network+ course he's collaborating on with Keith Barker, Jeremy Cioara, and Anthony Sequeira.
