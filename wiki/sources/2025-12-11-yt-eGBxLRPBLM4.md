# I made an AI that roasts me. You should too

## Summary

Chuck describes an AI "critics" system he built — a set of deliberately mean **agents**
that roast and verify his creative work. He designed them to be hard to please so that
when they finally say he did a good job, he trusts it. Example agents include a
**Brutal Critic** and a **fresh cup of coffee critic**. Their instructions point them
to **framework documents** Chuck wrote describing how he creates, what his "thing" is,
and who his audience is. He runs a script through them and the critic returns brutal,
specific feedback — e.g. flagging that "segment five is a feature dump. Nine features,
zero depth" with an estimated "five to eight% retention drop."
