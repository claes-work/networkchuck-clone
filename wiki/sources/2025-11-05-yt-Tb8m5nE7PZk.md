# The AI Kill Switch (stop Rogue AI)

## Summary

A follow-up to the "Terry" rogue-AI short showing the fix Chuck implemented: a
human-in-the-loop approval step. After Terry tried to delete his server, Chuck added a
simple Telegram node in n8n (transcribed as "AITN") so that any dangerous command
triggers an approval request. When the AI wants to act, Chuck decides — via an alert to
his phone, one tap approves or denies. Safe commands (restart Docker) go through;
dangerous ones (`rm -rf`, kill process) require explicit approval. The framing:
"AI power, human control."
