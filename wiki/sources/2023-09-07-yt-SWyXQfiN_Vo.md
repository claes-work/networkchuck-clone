# the BIGGEST server project they've ever done!! (45Drives)

## Summary

An unboxing video (episode one of a multi-part series) in which Chuck receives a massive
storage/server shipment from 45Drives — described by both Chuck and 45Drives as the biggest
project 45Drives has ever assembled. Over the course of the video he opens a large number of
boxes containing a server chassis (which holds the storage, processing, and RAM to run his
VMs), multiple Storinator storage servers of varying sizes (including "baby"/mini units),
stacks of 16 TB hard drives, and stacks of 480 GB SSDs. The new system is intended to replace
his current NAS and become his central server "for everything from virtual machines to video
editing" to whatever else he comes up with. The gear is destined for a dedicated server room
inside his studio, which he teases but refuses to fully reveal until later in the series. The
video establishes the scale of his growing homelab/production infrastructure; the actual
assembly and configuration are deferred to subsequent episodes.

## Key claims (dated — the build + scale)

All dated 2023-09-07 (paraphrase unless quoted):

- 45Drives sent Chuck a large multi-box shipment that he and they call the biggest project
  45Drives has ever done / the biggest NAS they've ever assembled.
- The shipment includes multiple Storinator storage servers of different sizes, including
  full-size units and smaller "baby"/mini Storinators.
- One box contained a server chassis that will hold the storage and also carries the
  processing and RAM needed to run his VMs.
- The shipment included many 16 TB hard drives — one box he opens holds twenty 16 TB drives.
- The shipment also included stacks of 480 GB SSDs.
- The gear ships with rack rails because the servers are going into his server room.
- His studio has a dedicated server room (teased/"behind the curtain" but not shown in this
  video).
- The new system will replace his current NAS and become his new server for everything from
  virtual machines to video editing to other projects; he plans to add it to his existing
  stack.
- This is the first video in a series; later episodes cover assembly, installing storage,
  configuration, and doing things 45Drives has never done before.
