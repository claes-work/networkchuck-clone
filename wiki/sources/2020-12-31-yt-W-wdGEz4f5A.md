## Summary

A short, informal year-end reflection and gratitude video closing out 2020. Chuck (Solo)
opens with a sip of coffee and a "goodbye 2020," acknowledging that the year held a lot of
bad but also a silver lining — situations that let people work from home, study more, earn
certifications, and get extra family time. He frames his own philosophy of choosing to
focus on the positive.

He then reviews his channel's 2020 performance (his biggest growth year to date), credits
the audience for enabling him to **quit his job and go full-time on YouTube this year**, and
thanks members, super chats, and commenters. He recaps the channel's "10 Days of Christmas"
giveaway (which stretched to 11 days), thanking each partner/vendor. He closes by pointing
viewers to a feedback survey (with a $100 giveaway for a random respondent) to help decide
the direction of his content in 2021, and signs off on his last video of the year.
