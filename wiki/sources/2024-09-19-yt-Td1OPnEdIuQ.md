# Do you know your protocols?

## Summary

A protocols quiz short: Chuck drags application-layer protocols onto their transport-layer protocol (TCP/UDP) and calls out each well-known port from memory. Framed as CCNA-exam prep, it ends with a sponsor plug for Boson ExSim-Max ("Bon XM Max"), the practice-exam tool he says he uses to prep for his own exams.
