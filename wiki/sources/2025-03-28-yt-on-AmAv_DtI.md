# EVERYTHING NEW with ChatGPT 4o Image Generation

## Summary

A rapid demo of new capabilities in ChatGPT's 4o image generation, contrasted against DALL-E 3. Chuck shows image restyling, previously-hard prompts (a wine glass full to the brim, a fully smashed burger), strong adherence to highly detailed prompts, character consistency across scenes, transparent PNG output, and converting personal photos into cartoon and other art styles (Japanese wood block, oil painting, abstract Picasso).

## Key points

- 2025-03-28: ChatGPT 4o image generation can restyle existing images, improving on DALL-E 3.
- 2025-03-28: It renders prompts DALL-E 3 failed at, e.g., a wine glass full to the brim and a completely smashed burger.
- 2025-03-28: It follows highly detailed prompts accurately.
- 2025-03-28: It maintains character consistency across different generated scenes, which ChatGPT could not do before.
- 2025-03-28: It can produce transparent PNGs and convert personal photos into cartoon and other styles (Japanese wood block, oil painting, abstract Picasso).
