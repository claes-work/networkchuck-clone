# One of these is NOT a Keyboard.

## Summary

Chuck reveals the new **Raspberry Pi 500 Plus** — a Raspberry Pi 5 hidden inside a keyboard. He contrasts it with the earlier Pi 500: the Plus doubles RAM to 16GB, ships with a 256GB internal NVMe SSD (no SD card needed, though the slot remains), and — the headline upgrade — swaps in a mechanical keyboard with full RGB. He frames it as a portable, full Linux dev environment you can throw in a backpack (needs only a monitor and mouse) usable for Docker, hosting websites, and building robots.
