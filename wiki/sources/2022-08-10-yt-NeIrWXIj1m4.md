# the 30 Day Cert Challenge

## Summary

A short motivation/call-to-action video in which Chuck Keith challenges viewers to
earn an IT certification in 30 days, starting the day the video drops. The framing is
community + accountability: the training is free (removing the "no time / no money"
excuse), and Chuck pledges to do the challenge alongside the audience, with members of
his team (Austin, Cameron) and even his wife roped in on camera. The specific target is
a Juniper associate-level certification — the JNCIA — using Juniper's free training
program at `juniper.net/network Chuck`. Chuck justifies the choice two ways: why get an
IT cert at all (certs prove verified, deep skills to employers and, in his opinion, can
be more valuable than degrees), and why Juniper specifically (networking is a vital IT
skill, and Juniper is a major networking vendor whose gear powers large companies and
much of the internet). He walks through the sign-up flow step by step, explains the exam
economics (a voucher assessment scoring 70%+ drops the exam price to $50, ~75% off), and
pitches the community layer: a dedicated Discord section, two Twitch live-study streams
where he builds his Juniper lab, possible Twitter Spaces, giveaways/swag, and a
completion sticker for anyone who submits proof of a cert earned during the 30 days. The
video is a sponsored collaboration with Juniper.
