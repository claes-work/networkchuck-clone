# someone could be watching you....

## Summary

A warning about cloud-connected IP / surveillance cameras bought off Amazon: because
most connect to the cloud, "the bad guys" can watch them from anywhere too. Chuck says
he spoke with a threat researcher in Romania who described dark-web markets selling
streams to home cameras. His fix: don't buy cloud cameras — host your own solution.
He recommends **Frigate**, a free, open-source, local, private AI-powered surveillance
system that works with any camera supporting **RTSP**. He found he lost nothing by
dropping the cloud and gained more: object detection, audio detection, and face
recognition all running locally, "nothing touching the internet."
