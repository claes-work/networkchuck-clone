# HUGE announcement!!

## Summary

A short announcement video in which Chuck Keith (NetworkChuck) reveals that he
quit his full-time job — his primary source of income — the previous Friday
(roughly one month before this video, so ~mid-June 2020) to pursue YouTube full
time. He frames the decision around his mission: helping people get started in
IT and stay encouraged to keep learning. The constraint that pushed the move was
that his day job prevented him from creating free courses on his YouTube channel
(he was then producing a free CCNA course). He also announces a partnership with
fellow YouTuber David Bombal ("Bomble" in the captions) under the banner "This
is IT" — a joint effort to publish full IT certification courses for free on
YouTube — plus a supporting paid membership ("This is IT" membership) at
thisisit.io with two tiers ("10x Engineer" and "This is IT Army") offering early
access, ad-free content, existing course libraries, live streams, and giveaways.
