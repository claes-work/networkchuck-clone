# What's next for NetworkChuck? *UPDATE* | CCNA | CCNP | Network Automation

## Summary

A short channel-direction update (late 2018) in which Chuck Keith recaps what he
has been doing and lays out where NetworkChuck and his own study focus are heading.
He reports that he has returned to Dallas, Texas after a stint of full-time family
travel across Europe, and that the main reason for coming back early was the
channel and his work — it was too hard to focus, study, and produce content while
traveling with three young kids. He shows his newly built recording studio, then
runs through what he is working on and excited about: a CCNP Collaboration course
(CIVND1 / first exam) for CBT Nuggets, an unfinished CCNP Routing & Switching goal
he plans to restart and complete, and — the thing he is most stoked about —
**network automation and network programmability**, on which he plans to make a lot
more content as he learns it, including a partnership with Cisco. He thanks his
community (nearly 44,000 subscribers at the time), asks viewers what certs and
topics they want, and closes with a ping-pong game against Jeremy Cioara.
