# Which Network Doesn't Belong?

## Summary

A CCNA-prep engagement quiz. Chuck poses two exam-style questions: (1) which network is not
defined by RFC 1918 (private address space), reasoning through the Class C, Class A 10.x, and
other options; and (2) in a controller-based network, which two protocols' functions are most
likely moved to a centralized controller — he uses a "brain transplant" analogy to land on
the EIGRP and OSPF routing protocols. The short closes with a Boson ExSim sponsor plug (the
tool he uses to prep for exams) and a 25% "12 days of deals" discount code.
