# i hired my replacement….kind of (the FUTURE of NetworkChuck)

## Summary

A non-tutorial "state of the channel" video in which Chuck Keith talks candidly about
NetworkChuck as a growing business/team rather than a one-man operation. He recaps his
2020 leap to full-time YouTube, his growth to 1M+ subscribers, and admits he "failed"
to finish his flagship free courses (CCNA, Linux for Hackers, Python) because he is the
bottleneck — one person can't produce at the volume and quality he wants. His solution:
scale into a team. He recounts hiring an admin and a video editor in 2021, adding a
second contract editor and a thumbnail graphic designer, and now hiring his brother
**Cameron** as a second on-camera content creator ("another me"). He also lays out the
future direction for 2022: bringing back the weekly Monday live stream (now as a podcast
with guests), focusing on community (Discord server), producing more content, and
investing in the NetworkChuck Premium membership site ($12/month). The framing that "I
feel like I'm talking about myself in third person — I mean NetworkChuck the company"
makes this a key bio artifact of the transition from person to brand/business.
