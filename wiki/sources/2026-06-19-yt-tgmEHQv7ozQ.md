# "shadow AI is terrifying"

## Summary

A Vanta-sponsored short in which Chuck frames "shadow AI" as a fast-spreading security risk: companies (his own included) are plugging in new AI tools, vendors, and integrations almost daily, and most teams have no idea what is running inside their own walls. The bulk of the runtime is a sponsor pitch for Vanta as an "agentic trust platform" that discovers unapproved AI tools and automates GRC/vendor assessment. Real security point is thin but genuine; second half is promo.

## Key points (paraphrase)

- 2026-06-19: Chuck says he adds new AI tools to his company roughly every week — now every day — and guarantees some are running that he never approved or even knows about.
- 2026-06-19: "Shadow AI" = every new AI tool/vendor/integration reaching into company data, something security never vetted; it spreads as fast as AI itself.
- 2026-06-19: Sponsor claim (Vanta): finds AI tools/vendors creeping into a company, runs a 24/7 GRC agent that assesses vendors, pulls evidence, and answers security questionnaires; claims to cut vendor-assessment time by 50% and cites 16,000+ customers.
