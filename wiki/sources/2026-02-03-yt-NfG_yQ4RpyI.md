# this NEW setting Locks Down WhatsApp

## Summary

Chuck highlights a new WhatsApp feature — "strict account settings" — a single button that instantly configures every privacy setting to lock down your account. He frames it as WhatsApp's "nuclear option" / "zero trust option" for high-risk users, motivated by spyware vendors (he names the NSO Group) that silently infect phones to steal messages, photos, and mic/camera access. He notes WhatsApp sued the NSO Group over targeting a small number of its users and won. He warns the setting is aggressive and changes how you use WhatsApp ("privacy does come at a cost"), so it's meant for high-risk/paranoid users, not everyone. To enable it: open WhatsApp → advanced settings → press the button for lockdown mode.
