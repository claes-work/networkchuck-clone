# Can You ACTUALLY Run AI on a Raspberry Pi?

## Summary

A short teaser asking whether you can actually run AI models on a Raspberry Pi.
Chuck says yes, thanks to the newly released **Raspberry Pi AI Hat Plus 2**, which
has 8 GB of onboard RAM — a big step up from the first version, which had none — and
finally enables running **local AI models** on a Raspberry Pi. He tries several models
of varying size at different speeds; some run, one is too big to run. Framed as a hook
toward a fuller build video.
