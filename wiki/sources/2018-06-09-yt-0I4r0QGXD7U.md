# I'm going to Cisco Live!!! - CiscoLIVE 2018 Orlando

## Summary

A short announcement video in which Chuck Keith (NetworkChuck) tells his audience
he is attending Cisco Live 2018 in Orlando, starting Monday June 11, 2018. He frames
it as a first-time milestone: he had been asking every company he worked at over the
prior ~6 years to send him, came close the previous year, but lost the trip when he
quit that job. This year Cisco itself invited him to come and preview their new
products. He promises to vlog and record everything for the channel, invites viewers
to follow him on Twitter, Instagram, and Facebook (all `@NetworkChuck`), and to find
him in person for a sticker. He solicits questions from viewers to ask the "greatest
engineers in the industry" on the floor. A key theme he wants to explore is the
future of networking — Software Defined Networking (SDN), Cisco DevNet, DevOps and
"NetDevOps" — reprising his "become a unicorn" advice that network engineers should
learn programming. He closes with an aside that the customer appreciation event is at
Universal Studios (home of Harry Potter world), noting he is a big Harry Potter fan.
