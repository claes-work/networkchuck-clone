# CCNA 200-301: Complete Video Training Course from CBT Nuggets

## Summary

This video is **largely a promotional announcement** for the newly released CBT Nuggets
CCNA 200-301 training course, tied to a 200k-subscriber giveaway (free CBT Nuggets
subscriptions and branded mugs, plus a 15% discount code and a 7-day free trial CTA — all
of which is pure promo and ignored here). The genuinely durable content is
**biographical**: Chuck confirms he **co-created and co-taught the CBT Nuggets CCNA
200-301 course** alongside CBT Nuggets trainers **Jeremy Cioara** and **Keith Barker**,
and that his specific contribution was the **Network Automation** domain (the final domain
of the new CCNA). He also restates his origin story — he got his start in networking by
watching Jeremy Cioara's CCNA course on CBT Nuggets.
